Sir MV RESEARCH LIBRARY

The Visvesvaraya library established in the memory of great indian Engineer Sir MV(1861-1965).Visvesvaraya was knighted as a Knight Commander of the Order of the Indian Empire (KCIE) by the British for his contributions to the public good. After India attained independence, he was awarded the nation's highest honour, the Bharat Ratna, in 1955 an honorary membership of London Institution of Civil Engineers.

Library established a network of creative units with in its complex wherein both the expert and the youthful novice have ample opertunity to develap their skills and enrich their knowledge.

The library has about 30000 books on various subject like Electronics,Electrical,Computer,Civil,Mechanical,Biomedicalengineering etc...
